SIMIA

Stratagem for Undestining
2021 — HD Video, CGI, Sound — 26 min
Simia: Stratagem for Undestining immerses its audience in a speculative future, centered around SIMIA, an advanced A.I. system that holds sway over a region reminiscent of the Middle East. The narrative weaves through the complexities of this new world order, where traditional economic models have been supplanted by innovative alternatives, and the concept of futurity is relentlessly questioned and reimagined.
