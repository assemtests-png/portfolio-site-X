Platform Poiesis

2021 — HD Video, Research — 17 min 32 sec
Technological platforms interfere with politics by generating new political relations and shifts in power. This project tells a historical account of how platform modelling capacity developed, from the Trans-Siberian Railway to China's Big Earth Data Platform.
