Everything Under Heaven

2021 — HD Video, CGI, Sound — 17 min 59 sec
Everything Under Heaven is a journey through Egypt's history, encapsulating time as a flux between the old and the new. The narrative focuses on the post-1952 era, where a newly formed state seeks to establish itself through grand infrastructural projects. It chronicles the clash and synthesis of different cultural elements and the contorting face of sovereignty.
